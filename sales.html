What is a Bridge Page — and why does it matter?

A bridge page is a page that sits between your opt-in page and the affiliate offer. Its job is to warm the lead — to take someone who just gave their email and prepare them to buy. Without a bridge page, cold traffic hits the offer page with no context. With one, they arrive already believing.
