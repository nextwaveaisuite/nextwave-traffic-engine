    ⚡ TrafficZest
    Not configured

    Point your TrafficZest campaign URL at a NextWave cloaked link. Each click is tracked, each opt-in is captured, and leads sync to Forge Mail automatically.

    Setup steps
    1.Create a cloaked link in the Link Cloaker tab
    2.Paste that cloaked link as your TrafficZest destination
    3.Visitors land on your funnel and opt in — lead is captured
    4.Lead syncs to Forge Mail — your sequence fires automatically
